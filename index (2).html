The club where business sense meets shipping code. We run DeFy, take ideas from notebook to stage, and hand juniors real responsibility from week one.

What we do

Not a "sit and listen" club.

Everything BiC runs is built and operated by students — you don't attend our events, you run them.

⚡

DeFy, our flagship

A two-round Web3 × business hackathon co-hosted with the DAO Community. Ideate online, then 24 hours of building on campus — with prize pools that make the all-nighter worth it.

◳

Business × tech, together

We don't stop at prototypes. Every build gets a pitch, a deck, and a hard look at whether anyone would actually pay for it. That combination is the whole point of BiC.

✦

Events all year

Speaker sessions, ideathons and TechnoVIT events — planned, sponsored and executed end-to-end by the core. The fastest way to learn ops is to own one.
